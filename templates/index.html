How to Get Your Google Timeline Data

  1. Open Google Maps on your mobile device.
  2. Tap the profile icon in the top right corner.
  3. Select "Your Timeline" from the menu.
  4. Tap the three dots ("...") in the top right corner.
  5. Choose "Location & privacy settings."
  6. Select "Export Timeline data."
  7. Send the file to yourself through your preferred method.
